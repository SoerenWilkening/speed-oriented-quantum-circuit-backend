Implement width-parameterized AND and OR operations in the C layer.

Purpose: AND and OR are more complex than NOT/XOR because they require output ancilla qubits. AND uses Toffoli (CCX) gates. OR implements De Morgan's law: a|b = ~(~a & ~b).

Output: Quantum-quantum AND/OR (Q_and, Q_or) and classical-quantum AND/OR (CQ_and, CQ_or) sequence generators.
</objective>
