Use a fresh qbool() as reject qubit, check appropriate board square, store validity[i] = NOT reject (CNOT from reject to validity, then X to invert).

         f. Uncompute oracle: oracle.inverse(wk, bk, wn, branch_reg)
         g. Undo height flip
         h. Undo branch register encoding (same emit_x pattern)
