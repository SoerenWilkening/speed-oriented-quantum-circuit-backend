<objective>
Implement R_A and R_B walk operators as standalone functions in chess_walk.py that compose apply_diffusion() at the correct depth levels.

Purpose: R_A and R_B are the two reflection operators whose product forms the quantum walk step U = R_B * R_A. This plan implements them as simple loops over apply_diffusion(), with R_A covering even depths (excluding root) and R_B covering odd depths plus root, following the Montanaro convention.

Output: r_a() and r_b() functions in chess_walk.py with structural/disjointness tests.

def R_A(self):  # line 1099
    for depth in range(0, self.max_depth + 1, 2):
        if depth == self.max_depth:
            continue  # Root always belongs to R_B

def R_B(self):  # line 1115
    for depth in range(1, self.max_depth + 1, 2):
